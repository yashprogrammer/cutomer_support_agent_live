# Banking Charges and Minimum Balance

## Minimum Balance Rules

- Urban branch savings account: minimum monthly average balance INR 10,000.
- Semi-urban branch savings account: minimum monthly average balance INR 5,000.
- Rural branch savings account: minimum monthly average balance INR 2,500.

## Non-Maintenance Charges

- Charge applies if average monthly balance is below required threshold.
- Typical non-maintenance fee: INR 350 + applicable taxes.
- Charges are posted at month-end billing cycle.

## SMS and Debit Card Charges

- SMS alert annual fee: INR 150 + taxes.
- Debit card annual maintenance fee: INR 250 + taxes.

## Account Closure Charges

- No closure fee after 14 days from account opening.
- Closure within first 14 days may attract INR 200 processing charge.
