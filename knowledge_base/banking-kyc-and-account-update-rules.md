# KYC and Account Update Rules

## KYC Update Frequency

- Full KYC update is required every 8 years for low-risk customers.
- Full KYC update is required every 2 years for high-risk customers.

## Accepted Address Proof

- Aadhaar card
- Passport
- Driving license
- Utility bill (not older than 3 months)

## Mobile Number or Email Update

- Updates can be done through branch or net banking.
- OTP verification is mandatory for all profile updates.
- Changes reflect within 30 minutes after successful verification.

## Name Correction Policy

- Minor spelling corrections are processed in 2 working days.
- Legal name change requires supporting legal documents and branch verification.
